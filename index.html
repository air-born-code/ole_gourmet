About Olé Gourmet

Olé Gourmet imports premium Spanish food products, specialising in artisan cocktail-size croquetas made for professional foodservice.

Our mission is to bring authentic Spanish flavours, reliable supply, and outstanding consistency to hotels, restaurants, and catering groups across Ireland.

We work with a leading Spanish manufacturer to ensure every croqueta delivers exceptional taste and performance in busy kitchens.
